Week Four: Rails!

I say this every week, but… this week flew by! As the title gave away, on Monday we started Ruby on Rails. We didn’t just jump into it though; the exercises and projects from the previous three weeks were building blocks for the powerful RoR. I’m now realizing the method to the madness. Having experienced creating projects with less structure, I know I like the more formatted style of Rails. I understand why Rails works the way it does.

Last night as I was listening to the “23 (CAKED UP Remix)” on SoundCloud, I recognized a very familiar URL pattern. I then found out that SoundCloud is based on this simple setup:

Internet -> Web -> App (Rails) -> Data (MySQL)

Hey, I understand that! I’m not completely used to Rails yet, but it’s coming together.
